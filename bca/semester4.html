Web Programming Lab


India is a large country. Different regions observe variations in climate. The spoken language of one state is quite different from that of another. They wear different types of garments. They celebrate different festivals and perform varied religious rites. People belonging to diverse cultures belong to different religious faiths. In spite of these diversities, Indians feel a sense of unity and oneness among them. Thus, we conclude that India is a land of Unity in Diversity.

  1. All the headings should be H2 and green colour.
  2. Main heading should be H1 and centre aligned.
  3. The background should be yellow colour.
  4. There are 10 paragraphs so each of them should be made using P tag.
  5. The Introduction and Conclusion paragraphs should have “Times New Roman” font, the size should be 12 and colour should be blue.
  6. All the remaining paragraphs text should be pink and magenta coloured in an alternate way.
  7. There should be one meaningful picture in the web page with specific dimension.
